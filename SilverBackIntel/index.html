How It Works

One conversation generates intelligence for your entire industry.

Silverback acquires raw behavioural data from independent AI-powered consumer engagement platforms. Hundreds of thousands of UK consumers complete weekly check-ins with a conversational AI coach, answering questions about their habits, preferences, triggers, and brand relationships. Every conversation is processed through our intelligence pipeline and delivered to your dashboard within 48 hours.

Your competitors are guessing. Your consumers are talking to us.

Traditional market research gives you what people say they do. Purchase data gives you what they bought, but not why. Social listening gives you curated performance, not reality.

Silverback gives you honest, unguarded behavioural truth from hundreds of thousands of verified, demographically profiled UK consumers who voluntarily share detailed information about their habits every single week.

When someone tells our AI coach "I had three Red Bulls this week because I was exhausted from working late, I usually drink Monster but they were out", that single sentence generates intelligence for energy drinks, caffeine, workplace stress, brand loyalty, competitor switching, and occasion mapping.

You are not buying a survey. You are licensing access to a living, breathing intelligence ecosystem that gets smarter every week.

Intelligence Pipeline

From raw conversation to your dashboard in under 48 hours.

01

Acquire

Raw conversational data acquired from independent AI engagement platforms. Hundreds of thousands of verified weekly interactions. 12-20 datapoints per conversation across all categories simultaneously.

02

Anonymise & Classify

PII stripped at source. k-anonymity and differential privacy applied. NLP extraction classifies signals into your sector's intelligence categories. Entity recognition identifies brands, products, and occasions.

03

Enrich & Model

Cross-referencing against longitudinal history. Cohort pattern detection. Predictive modelling for churn, switching, and demand. Anomaly detection flags emerging trends before they appear in sales data.

04

Deliver

Processed intelligence served to your personalised dashboard. Weekly automated reports to your inbox. Custom alerts for threshold breaches. Natural language query engine for ad-hoc analysis. API access for BI integration.

Compliance & Data Governance

GDPR compliant by architecture, not by policy.

Anonymised at Source

All personally identifiable information is stripped before entering the Silverback pipeline. Intelligence products contain no individual-level data. Subjects cannot be re-identified from output intelligence.

Aggregated Intelligence

You license processed, aggregated behavioural patterns, not personal data. k-anonymity enforcement ensures minimum cohort sizes. Individual GDPR deletion requests do not affect aggregated intelligence products.

Arm's Length Architecture

Your contract is exclusively with Silverback Intelligence. No direct relationship with upstream data sources. Complete commercial and operational separation. Clean audit trail at every layer.

Consent by Design

Multi-layer consent architecture from platform sign-up through enhanced data engagement. Users voluntarily opt in to broader lifestyle data sharing. Incentivised participation ensures data quality and ethical compliance.
