Privacy Policy

Who We Are

Silverback Intelligence Inc ("Silverback", "we", "our") is a specialist behavioural data brokerage headquartered in Nassau, Bahamas. We acquire anonymised consumer data from independent third-party platforms, process it through proprietary analytics, and license the resulting intelligence to enterprise clients.

What Data We Process

Silverback does not collect data directly from consumers. We purchase anonymised, structured behavioural datasets from independent AI-powered engagement platforms under commercial supply agreements. All personally identifiable information is stripped at source by the originating platform before any data enters the Silverback pipeline.

The data we process includes:

  • Anonymised behavioural signals (product usage, brand preferences, satisfaction ratings)
  • Aggregated demographic segmentation (age bands, gender, postcode district)
  • Classified sentiment and trend data
  • Longitudinal behavioural patterns across anonymised cohorts

We do not process names, email addresses, phone numbers, IP addresses, payment details, or any other personally identifiable information.

Legal Basis for Processing

All upstream data collection by our source platforms operates under explicit, informed user consent compliant with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Users of source platforms provide multi-layer consent covering the collection, anonymisation, and licensing of aggregated behavioural intelligence to enterprise clients.

How We Use the Data

Anonymised data is processed through our NLP and analytics pipeline to produce structured intelligence products for enterprise clients. These products include sector-specific dashboards, automated reports, trend analysis, and custom research commissions. All outputs are aggregated and cannot be used to identify any individual.

Data Sharing

Intelligence products are licensed exclusively to contracted enterprise clients under sector-specific agreements. We do not sell, share, or distribute raw data. We do not share data with government agencies, advertising platforms, social media companies, or any third party outside of our contracted client relationships.

Data Retention

Anonymised behavioural data is retained for the duration of the relevant client agreement to enable longitudinal analysis. Upon contract expiry or termination, client-specific intelligence products are archived and access is revoked. Aggregated, non-attributable trend data may be retained for internal research purposes.

Your Rights

Because Silverback processes only anonymised, aggregated data that cannot be linked to any identifiable individual, individual data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure) under UK GDPR do not apply to our processed datasets. If you are a user of one of our upstream source platforms and wish to exercise your data rights, please contact that platform directly. Upon withdrawal of consent at the source platform, that user's data is excluded from all future Silverback processing.

Data Governance Framework

Architecture-Level Compliance

Silverback Intelligence operates a compliance-by-design data architecture. Privacy and data protection are enforced at the infrastructure level, not by policy alone. The following principles are embedded into every stage of the intelligence pipeline.

Anonymisation at Source

All personally identifiable information is stripped by the originating data platform before any data enters the Silverback pipeline. Silverback never receives, stores, or processes PII. This separation is enforced at the application layer and validated by automated compliance checks on every incoming data batch.

k-Anonymity Enforcement

All intelligence products enforce minimum cohort sizes across every reporting dimension (demographic, regional, temporal). No output can be generated for cohorts below the minimum threshold. This ensures that no individual can be re-identified from any intelligence product, regardless of the specificity of the query.

Consent Architecture

Upstream data platforms operate multi-layer consent frameworks compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. User consent covers:

  • Collection of behavioural data for commercial research purposes
  • Anonymisation and processing by third-party analytics providers
  • Licensing of aggregated intelligence to enterprise clients

Users can withdraw consent at any time via the originating platform. Upon withdrawal, that user's data is excluded from all future Silverback processing within the next batch cycle.

Arm's Length Structure

Enterprise clients contract exclusively with Silverback Intelligence. There is no direct contractual, financial, or data relationship between enterprise clients and upstream data sources. Silverback operates as an independent data broker with its own commercial agreements, margin structure, and client relationships. This architecture ensures complete separation at every layer.

Audit and Transparency

Enterprise clients receive full audit rights to verify active user counts, data methodology, and anonymisation procedures. Monthly reconciliation reports are provided as standard. Every data transformation in the pipeline is logged and traceable.

Regulatory Compliance

The entire Silverback data architecture has been reviewed and validated by independent legal counsel for compliance with:

  • UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)
  • Data Protection Act 2018
  • Applicable sector-specific advertising and promotion regulations
